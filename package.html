Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked question and some misgiving from customers.

  • What should we prepare before we book travel package?

    Just prepare your passport, we will solve all the required documents such insurance, permit and visas.

  • Can we book our own hotel?

    Yes, you could book your own hotel and you can follow the events we prepared. But you should prepare your own transport and assemble with team on time.

  • Do we have any halal food choices?

    Yes, we do have a halal food choices during the trip. But you will need to go to the different restaurant for your meals.

  • What if I dont know the local language?

    No worries, our tour guide knows the multiple languages for customers to communicate and bargain.

  • Do we need to prepare our own SIM card for travel.

    No, when we arrive at our destination, we will buy a SIM card for all our customers, and we will record your SIM card number in case of emergency.
